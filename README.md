# Salaries_Dataset

1-Project Overview:

This project focuses on analyzing a salary dataset to extract meaningful insights about employee compensation, job roles, and departmental trends.

2-Objective:
            Analyze salary distribution and trends,
            Identify highest and lowest paid employees,
            Explore job title patterns and frequency

3-Tools & Technologies:
                      Python ,
                      Pandas ,
                      Jupyter Notebook (.ipynb)

4-Dataset

          The dataset contains employee details such as:

          Employee Name,
          Job Title,
          Base Pay, Overtime Pay, Total Pay,
          Benefits and Department information
5-Key Analysis Performed:
                        Data cleaning (handled missing values, removed unnecessary columns),
                        Checked dataset shape, datatypes, and null values,
                        Found top 5 most common job titles,
                        Analyzed average salary by year and job role,
                        Identified highest paid employee and salary trends

6-Key Insights-
              Certain job roles dominate salary distribution,
              Significant variation exists in pay across departments,
              High-paying roles are concentrated in specific job titles
